Formula 1 Recap Studio

Convert structured race data into an explorable broadcast-style recap. The platform includes live order playback, tyre strategy, pit history, and race event overlays in one telemetry-focused UI.

Core Features

  • Live Timing TowerPosition, interval, and tyre compound updates during playback.
  • Track VisualizationAnimated car positions mapped from extracted GPS traces or synthetic pathing.
  • Strategy PanelsTyre stints, pit stop history, and race statistics side-by-side.
  • Control SurfacePlay, seek, skip, and speed controls aligned with race lap progression.
